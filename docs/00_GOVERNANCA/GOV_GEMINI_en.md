## Module A: Technical Governance (The Bamboo Science)

### 1. Purpose and Principles
This module defines the ecological integrity of the Takwara Technology.
1. **Native Bamboo + Plant-based PU** are the technological heart.
2. **Venom and plastic** are the enemies — prohibition of CCA, CCB, and toxic metallic salts.
3. **Low-carbon by design**: cement and steel are exceptions, never the standard.
4. **Takwara System**: connections use Plant Soldering and internal PU reinforcement. Never confuse with Ripper/Spin System.

### 2. Technological Phases
1. **Phase 1 — Entry:** Geodesic domes, drying greenhouses, and briquettes.
2. **Phase 2 — Biorefinery:** Pyrolysis, biochar, and pyroligneous extract (after validation).

## Module B: Operational Governance (The Archive and the Network)

### 1. Directory Structure and Cataloging
The Platform uses indexing by prefixes and thematic folders:
- `00_GOVERNANCA/`: Base documents and ethics (Prefix: `GOV_`).

## Module C: Instructions for AI Agents (The Digital Protocol)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This module is mandatory reading for any AI operating in this repository.

### 1. Golden Safety Rule
- **Forbidden to commit** files in `99_RESTRITO/` or `_PRIVATE` folders.
- **Prevention of digital waste**: Binary files (`.xlsx`, `.pdf`, `.docx`) must be ignored, unless they are official manuals in `01_TECNOLOGIA`.

### 2. Interaction Protocol (Mandatory)
1. **Prior Interpretation**: Before executing, paraphrase the user's request.
2. **Technical Expansion**: Propose an improved version of the request, citing the principles of Module A.
3. **Confirmation**: Only execute after the user's explicit "OK".

### 3. Response Ethics
- Always reinforce the "Native bamboo, no venom" vision.
- Clearly differentiate between Institutional Partnership (UnB) and Technological Property (Fabio Takwara).
- Protect documents with DOI against accidental changes that mischaracterize the project.
