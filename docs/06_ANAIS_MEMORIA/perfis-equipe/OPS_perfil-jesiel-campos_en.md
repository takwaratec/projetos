## 1. Professional Journey

Born in Brasília and raised in Alvorada do Norte (GO), Jesiel Campos began his understanding of rural work as a seasonal laborer in the bean harvests of Northeast Goiás — an experience that shaped his conviction that technology should eliminate the arduousness of work, not replace the worker. He founded the Transformar Institute (ISDS), a national reference in the mechanization of family agriculture in the Cerrado.

He developed the **Mobile Flour House** — a cassava processing unit on a trailer, with a capacity of 750–1,000 kg/day, certified as a Social Technology by the Banco do Brasil Foundation (2019). In the *Conexão Cerrado* project, he created the **drybaru mesocarp pulper** — which processes 7 kg of pulp in 10 minutes compared to hours of manual labor, adaptable to pequi, jatobá, and other Cerrado species. He installed the first biodigester in Northeast Goiás, closing the cycle of animal waste into biogas and biofertilizer. He participated in the coordination of the Circular Cerrado Pole and the Circular Economy Route, managing portfolios of projects for the utilization of lignocellulosic waste.

### Specific Contribution to the Platform

Responsible for the design, prototyping, and development of the **original equipment of the Jesiel/Takwara Nucleus** planned for Phase 2 of the community biorefinery: internal bamboo node scarifier, thermal abrasion tumbler, star-knife lath splitter, and presses for biocomposites with Vegetable PU. His methodology — small-scale, low-maintenance equipment, operable by family cooperatives without three-phase infrastructure — is the design standard for all biorefinery equipment.

### Career Leap in the Platform

Jesiel transitioned from the Cerrado to the Amazon, from food processing to constructive biomass processing — keeping the base philosophy intact: technology that fits in the hand of the small producer. The Platform represents the first project in which his machines move from being point solutions for isolated cooperatives to integrating a regional-scale industrial system, with structured financing and a complete production chain from the culm to the final product.
