## About the Project

The **Regenerative Amazon Platform** articulates four complementary fronts
of circular bioeconomy in the Legal Amazon:

| Core | Responsible | Focus |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| 🎋 **Bamboo & Biorefinery** | Fabio Resck Takwara | Pyrolysis, biochar, SMGA, carbon credit, geodesics, Vegetable PU |
| ⚙️ **Machines & Equipment** | Jesiel | Social technology kits, kilns, presses |
| 🏗️ **Bioconstruction** | Filemon Tiago | HIS, adobe, hybrid systems |
| 👩 **Women & Management** | Female Core | Cooperatives, handicrafts, community governance |

## Repository Structure (Protocol 5.1)

The organization follows logical silos to ensure technical integrity and Advocacy rigor:

- [**00_GOVERNANCA/**](docs/00_GOVERNANCA/): Authorship management, IP, correlation matrix, and data sharing technical notes.
- [**01_TECNOLOGIA_TAKWARA/**](docs/01_TECNOLOGIA_TAKWARA/): Phytoremediation manuals, structural connections, and base technologies.
- [**02_PESQUISA_DESENVOLVIMENTO/**](docs/02_PESQUISA_DESENVOLVIMENTO/): R&D reports (Vegetal PU, Biorefinery, SMEGA, Biochar).
- [**05_ADVOCACY_COP30/**](docs/05_ADVOCACY_COP30/): Strategic dossiers and whistleblower documents for political advocacy.
- [**06_ANAIS_MEMORIA/**](docs/06_ANAIS_MEMORIA/): Master documents of Platform v5.1, team profiles, and historical records.
- [**07_BLOG_MEDIUM/**](docs/07_BLOG_MEDIUM/): Complete archive of articles published on Medium.
- [**08_PROJETOS_ENGENHARIA/**](docs/08_PROJETOS_ENGENHARIA/): Technical memorials of equipment and social technology patents.
- [**99_RESTRITO/**](docs/99_RESTRITO/): (Private Access) Strategy, data triage, and the Author's personal network.
